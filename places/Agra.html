Agra

Agra is the town of the famous Taj Mahal. You can get here relatively easily via road (taxi) or train.

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a mausoleum built by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan for his favourite wife. It was completed in 1643.

Be aware there are very large crowds here, mostly national visitors. I would recommend arranging a guide beforehand. Also be aware that freelance photographers might approach you. I would recommend asking for the price if you are interested in their service. Personally, I thought it was nice.

Agra Fort

Agra Fort is not too far from the Taj Mahal. It is worth visiting. It has amazing gardens and Mughal architecture.
