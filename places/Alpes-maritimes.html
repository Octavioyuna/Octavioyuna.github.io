Alpes-Maritimes

Alpes-Maritimes is a department of France on the coast bordering Italy.

Nice

Nice has a reputation for attracting many notable painters, such as Marc Chagall, Henri Matisse, Niki de Saint Phalle and Arman. Chagall and Matisse have museums with their art.

The Nice Carnival (French: Carnaval de Nice) is one of the world's major carnival events.

Menton

Menton is a town near the border with Italy. It is famous for the Menton Lemon Festival (Fête du Citron).

Cannes

Cannes is famous for its international film festival. The Palais des Festivals et des Congrès has a red carpet and Allée des Étoiles where famous actors have hand prints.
