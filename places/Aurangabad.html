Aurangabad

Aurangabad is the closest major city to the Ellora Caves.

Ellora Caves

The Ellora Caves are one of the most impressive and culturally interesting places I have ever been to. They represent the history of a blend of 3 asian religions: Buddhism, Hinduism and Jainism. Each of the religions has a set of caves and monuments with its own characteristic art and architecture. It is a UNESCO world heritage site.

The first set of caves contains paintings depicting events in the life of Gautama Buddha. The Hindu caves are the largest and contain sculptures of Hindu deities, including Shiva.
