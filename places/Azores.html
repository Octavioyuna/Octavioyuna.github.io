Azores

The Azores archipelago is composed of nine volcanic islands. They are a great place for hiking, bathing and enjoying nature.

Flores Island and Corvo Island

Flores Island (Ilha das Flores) was personally my favourite island because of its natural beauty. Corvos island is accessible from here - it has a big crater where one can hike. It is advisable to hire a car with a lot of anticipation if possible.

In the spring/summer months, the island is covered in hydrangeas (commonly named the hortensia), which make it very picturesque.

Portuguese man of war. These are animals in a group called hydrozoa. They related to (but not the same as) jellyfish. They can sting with serious consequences so they are typically removed from the water in the bathing areas of the coast.
