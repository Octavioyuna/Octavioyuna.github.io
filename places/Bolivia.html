Bolivia

Bolivia is a geographically diverse country. It is famous in part for its pre-European culture.

La Paz

La Paz is the highest capital city in the world. Since 2014 the city had a new urban transportation system based on gondolas. This is a great way to see the city. It works with a card which you can put money on.

The inner town has a lot of character. There are some traditional shops called "tiendas esotericas" whcih sell articles related to traditional local beliefs.

The traffic can be quite bad from the airport to the town.

La Paz panorama as seen from the gondolas. This system was introduced to the city in 2014.

Isla del Sol

This is an island on Lake Titicaca. It contains a beautiful landscape and some Inca ruins. You can get around by walking or by boat.

Salar de Uyuni and surrounding area

The Salar de Uyuni is the largest salt flat in the world. When rain covers it, it also becomes the world's largest mirror. The typical tour is a 3-4 day drive through the salt plain and to the lakes and desert. This had some of the most amazing landscapes I'd ever seen. It is a long car ride, but worth it, in my opinion.

Animals

The fauna is absolutely stunning. In the areas surrounding the salt flat you can see camelids (lamas, alpacas, vicuñas, etc), viscachas and flamingos.
