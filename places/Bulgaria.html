Bulgaria

Bulgaria has a very many beautiful sites and a rich history. Here are our favourite sites in each town.

Sofia

Sofia has great food and sightseeing. There are Roman ruins in the center of the city, where an excavation is underway.

Rila Monastery

This Monastery is impressive and beautiful. The museum has a very good exhibit of the history, whcih is interesting and very rich.

Plovdiv

Plovdiv is also very beautiful - there are remains of Roman civilization around the town.
