Bulgaria

Bulgaria is cheaper than most European countries so you can get around even on a budget. It has a rich history and is great for sightseeing.

Sofia

Sofia has great food and sightseeing. There are Roman ruins around the city, most notably in the city center at the Sveta Petka, a small, ancient church, where an excavation is underway. The site is open to visitors.

Rila Monastery

This Monastery is impressive and well maintained. The museum has a very good exhibit of its history, which is interesting and very rich.

Plovdiv

Plovdiv has remains of Roman civilization around the town. There are historical buildings in the town which have been maintained.

The ethnographic museum has a very good exhibit and is housed in a picturesque historical building.
