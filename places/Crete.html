Crete

Crete is the largest Greek island and was the centre of the Minoans, Europe's first advanced civilization. It is famous for the Minoan Palace in Knossos.

Knossos Palace

This Bronze Age archaeological site is very well maintained and is one of the most famous examples of Minoan civilisation.
