Dublin

Dublin has many interesting museums and sites as well as a very good night life.

Trinity College

You can get a guided tour here. It also houses the Book of Kells, an early manuscript Gospel book in Latin, containing the four Gospels of the New Testament.

The Long Room. This is the library of the university containing many historical books and artefacts.

Exterior.

Temple bar

There is an agglomeration of pubs/bars in this area.

Guinness Storehouse

This is a museum which explains the process of making Guinness stout beer and it also has a hall where traditional Irish dancing is performed.
