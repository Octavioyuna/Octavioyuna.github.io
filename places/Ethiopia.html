Ethiopia

Ethiopia is a fascinating place for both nature and culture enthsiasts.

Addis Ababa

Addis Ababa is the capital of Ethiopia. The museum is a must for anthropologists as it contains two of the most important hominid fossils.

Ardi (Ardipithecus ramidus).

Australopithecus afarensis specimen called "Lucy." .

Afar

The Afar region has some amazing places of natural beauty in the Danakil Depression, such as the volcano Erta Ale and the geothermal Dallol area, which has a hellish yet beautiful landscape.

Erta Ale is a continuously active basaltic shield volcano. Erta Ale means "smoking mountain".
