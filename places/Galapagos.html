Galapagos

Galapagos is stunning, with animals such as sea lions, iguanas and giant turtles. Each island has its own flora and Fauna. We went to Isla San Cristobal and Isla Santa Cruz. The best thing was seing the animals.

A unique ecosystem

Galapagos is especially interesting for biologists, since it has been geographically isolated and therefore subjected to unique evolutionary selection pressures. Only life capable of traversing the vast stretch of water have flourished on the islands. That is why there are no mammals but a few reptilian species managed to make it across via vegetation rafts which washed ashore from the continental mainland.
