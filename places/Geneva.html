Geneva

Geneva is an international city with a very interestig character.

CERN

The European Organization for Nuclear Research or CERN (Conseil européen pour la recherche nucléaire) is an international particle physics research lab. It has a very interesting exhibit, the Globe of Science and Innovation, which opened in late 2005. To see the actual particle accelerator it is advised to shcedule it in advance.

Palace of Nations

The Palace of Nations (Palais des Nations) is the United Nations (UN) Office at Geneva. There are open tours for the public.

Lake Geneva

Lake Geneva (Lac Léman) has many activities such as bathing, taking out motor boats, windsurfing.

City center

  • Reformation Wall (Mur des Réformateurs) and the park next to it
  • Maison Tavel

Food

  • Lakeside restaurants have “Filets de perches” fish from the lake e.g. La Belotte, La Perle du Lac
  • Traditional Swiss such as fondue - Restaurant Les Armures

Lake Leman area

  • Lausanne - similar lakeside activities as Geneva
