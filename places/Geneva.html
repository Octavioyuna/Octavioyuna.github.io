Geneva

Geneva is an international city with a very interesting character. It hosts many international organisations, including the United Nations headquarters in Europe.

Lake Geneva

Lake Geneva (Lac Léman) has many activities such as bathing, taking out motor boats, windsurfing. The famous Jet d'Eau is a powerful water fountain that dominates the skyline.

CERN

The European Organization for Nuclear Research or CERN (Conseil européen pour la recherche nucléaire) is an international particle physics research lab. It has a very interesting exhibit, the Globe of Science and Innovation, which opened in late 2005. To see the actual particle accelerator it is advised to shcedule it in advance.

Palace of Nations

The Palace of Nations (Palais des Nations) is the United Nations (UN) Office at Geneva. There are open tours for the public.
