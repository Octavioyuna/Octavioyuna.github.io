Petra

Petra is a historic and archaeological city inhabited from as early as 7000 BC and the Nabataeans. The Nabataeans were very good water infrastructure engineers and this allowed them to sustain their civilisation. The site is quite large with many temples and structures and includes sites which you can hike to in a few hours.

Al-Khazneh "The Treasury"). This building was originally a tomb. The origial levels are umderground.

Gulf of Aqaba

The Gulf of Aqaba or Gulf of Eilat is a large gulf at the northern tip of the Red Sea, quite easily reachable from Aqaba. It is a great place for beaches and diving/snorkelling.
