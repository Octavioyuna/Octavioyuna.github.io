La Palma

La Palma is the fifth largest island of the Canary Islands. In September to December 2021 an unexpected volcanic eruption occured in Cumbre Vieja.

Caldera de Taburiente National Park and surrounding area

The Caldera de Taburiente is a large crater which dominates the northern part of the island. There are many beautiful hikes which can be done here as well as several points of interest. The Roque de los Muchachos is the highest point on La Palma, at a height of 2,426 meters. The Roque de los Muchachos Observatory are located here.

Porís de Candelaria

Porís de Candelaria is a small fishing village hidden in the cliffs. It is a little difficult to get to but you can access the parking with a good "normal" car as long as you go slowly and carefully.

Santa Cruz de La Palma and Tazacorte

Santa Cruz de La Palma is one of the main towns on the island. Tazacorte is a nice town on the other side of the island. Both have nice beaches where it is possible to bathe, but Tazacorte has better weather due to micro climate. To eat we recommend the restaurant Chipi Chipi.
