Liechtenstein

Liechtenstein is a European microstate between between Austria and Switzerland. The country is known for its medieval castles, Alpine landscapes and villages; it is a great place for hiking since there are many interconnected trails.

The flora and fauna is also very beautiful. One can see wild marmots in the countryside.

Vaduz is the capital. It has many interesting museums.
