Lisbon

Lisbon is the capital of Portugal. It is recognized as an alpha-level global city.

Sintra

Sintra contains several castles/forts/palaces on hills. They are very impressive. Beware this area is extremely touristy and the roads are narrow and very esily prone to traffic.

This is a medieval Moorish castle very near to the Pena palace.

This is a very interesting and well-designed aquarium. There is also a cable car and a science museum very close to it.
