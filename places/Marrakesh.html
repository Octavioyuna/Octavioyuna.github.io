Marrakesh

Marrakesh is the fourth-largest city in Morocco. It is very interesting because of its historical sites and markets, which are very lively. Zagora, the desert town is a few hours away so it is possible to visit the Sahara desert.

Berbers were native to North Africa and the Sahara before the arrival of Islam which brought Arabic with it. Arabic language and Arab culture spread among the Berbers, leading to the adoption of Arabic as the primary language.

Moroccan cuisine is very rich; the tagine is especially famous. Tajine is both the name of the dish and the conical clay pot in which it is traditionally cooked; there are many types.
