Moscow

Moscow is a very beautiful and interesting city. The architecture especially is very impressive. A lot can be seen in the Red Square which is located in the historic centre, in the eastern walls of the Kremlin. The metro stations are very elegant and clean.

Red Square

This is the historic center. Lots of the most famous landmarks are located here, such as the Tomb of Lenin. It is possilbe to go inside the tomb.

Kremlin

The Kremlin is the fortified complex in the center of the city. It includes five palaces, four cathedrals, and the enclosing Kremlin Wall with Kremlin towers.

The cathedrals inside are very beautiful and impressive. They are worth visiting.

Science Museum

For people interested in science this is a must and is on a par with the sience museums of other capitals. There are detailed exhibits of the Soviet space program. For example an exhibit of Belka and Strelka, the dogs which were among the first ever animals into space. They spent a day in space aboard Korabl-Sputnik 2 (Sputnik 5) on 19 August 1960 before safely returning to Earth.
