Norway

Norway is famous for its fjords and many other natural wonders. The country is extremely organised and travel is comfortable.

Bergen

Bergen is a port city that hosts an international airport. There is a beautiful forest with a footpath overlooking the city.

Flam is a town on the railway that cuts across the country. It is a good place to visit fjords.
