Paris

Paris is one of the most visited cities in the world and one of the major cities in Europe. It is extremely rich in culture and impressive architecture. Most of the mainstream tourism is localised to certain hotspots which are justifiably busy. However they are worth visiting in spite of the crowds. Furthermore, you can still get a very nice feel of the city by going to less touristy places.

Notre Dame Cathedral

Tour Eiffel

Be prepared for large crowds here, depending on the season. You can get to the higher level either by elevator or on foot.

Jardins de Luxembourg

These are some very beautiful large gardens with scenic fountains, pools, classical statues and buildings. It is definitely worth a visit.

The Louvre

The Louvre is an enormous museum. Several days can be spent here. There is a collection of classical art in wings according to time and geographical origin. Home of the famous Mona Lisa, arguably the most famous painting in human history. You may see a crowd next to it and it is smaller than you might expect.
