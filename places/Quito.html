Quito

You can spend several days here as there is a lot to see. My favourite things were La Mitad del Mundo and the art museums. There is significnat poverty in the inner city unfortunately.

La Mitad del Mundo

La Mitad del Mundo has the physical and geographical qeuator. There is a series of monuments and mueseums that I highly recommend. There are 2 sites. One with the monument and museums, and another with the physical equator where they perform physical experiments which prove you are indeed at the exact equator. These are fascinating and I hesitate to spoil it with detail. Here there is also an anthropological/ethnographic museum where you can do a guided tour. I found it extremely interesting and enjoyable.

La Capilla del Hombre

If you are more into art, I would very highly recommend La Capilla del Hombre which has an enormous collection of paintings by Oswaldo Guayasamín. Oswaldo Guayasamín's house is right next to it with a collection of art from many painters.
