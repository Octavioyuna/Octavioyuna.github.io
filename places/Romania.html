Romania

Romania has a rich history and many interesting places. It has several famous churches and castles, such as Bran castle a.k.a. "Dracula's castle in Transylvania.

Maramureș

Maramureș is the northern region of Romania bordering Ukraine. Notable sites include the Merry Cemetery in Săpânța. The Wooden Churches of Maramureş are a UNESCO world heritage site.

Transylvania

Transylvania is famous for being the home of Dracula. The character is loosely based on the historical figure of Vlad III, commonly known as Vlad the Impaler or Vlad Dracula, who might have had some association with Bran castle.
