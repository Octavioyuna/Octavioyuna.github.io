Sarajevo

Sarajevo has a unique history that makes for a unique mixture of cultures.

The city suffered a lot in the the Bosnian war of 1992-1995. There is a Sarajevo Tunnel museum which is the preserved tunnel from the 1993 Siege of Sarajevo in the midst of the Bosnian War. It was used to transport critical supplies to the city.
