Sicily

Sicilian ceramic heads are a common sight everywhere on the island. They are locally known as “teste di moro“. The original folk tale behind the ceramic heads comes from Palermo and tells of a Moor merchant who falls in love with a beautiful local girl. They are made in pairs.
