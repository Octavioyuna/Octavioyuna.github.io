South Tyrol

South TyrolSouth (German: Südtirol; Italian: Alto Adige) is a province in Northern Italy. It is great for hiking and enjoying mountain activities.

Bolzano and hiking in the region

From Bolzano and the villages in the north there are many hiking trails which go through the mountains and lakes.

The Messner Mountain Museum is particularly interesting. There are actually 5 of them.

Verona

Verona has lots of interesting historical and cultural sites. One of the most famous is Juliet's Balcony in Casa di Giulietta (Juliet's House), a stone balcony, said to have inspired Shakespeare..
