Stresa

Stresa is a town on Lake Maggiore (Italian: Lago Maggiore). From there you can easily access the Borromean Islands: Isola Bella, Isola dei Pescatori, Isola Madre.

There is a ferry service between the islands.

Isola Bella

Isola Bella has the Palazzo Borromeo. It has a museum inside and a beautiful garden complex next to it.
