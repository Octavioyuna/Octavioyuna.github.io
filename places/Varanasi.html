Varanasi

Varanasi is a city famous for being a Hindu spiritual center and the ghats on the Ganges. The nearby town of Sarnath is notable for the park where Gautama Buddha first taught the Dharma.

Ghats and Ganges bank

The ghats on the Ganges are very beautiful. However, some of the streets leading up to them in the city center are quite unhygenic.

University

The University has some very nice gardens and a very good atmosphere.
