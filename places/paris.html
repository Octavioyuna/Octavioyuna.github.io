Paris

Paris is one of the most visited cities in the world. However, a lot of it is localised to the touristic spots and you can still get a very nice feel of the city by going to less touristy places. However, the main sites are magnificent albeit quite touristy.

Note Dame Cathedral

Worth a visit

Tour Eiffel

Be prepared for large crowds here but worth the wait!

Jardins de Luxembourg

These are some very beautiful large gardens with scenic fountains, pools, classical statues and buildings. It is definitely worth a visit.

The Louvre

The Louvre is an enormous museum. Several days can be spent here. There is a collection of classical art in wings according to time and geographical origin. Mona Lisa is here.
